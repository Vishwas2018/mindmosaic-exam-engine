FAQ

Questions parents ask us.

Something else on your mind? Write to hello@mindmosaic.app — a human replies within a day.

Which year levels and exams are supported? ＋

MindMosaic is built for primary learners in Years 3–6, across NAPLAN, ICAS, Selective entry, Singapore Math and Olympiad pathways. Grade 3 and Grade 5 content is live today, with more year levels rolling out.

How are the weekly plans generated? ＋

From your child's actual results. Every response updates a per-skill mastery model; each week the engine selects 3–5 priority skills with daily minutes. Every recommendation traces back to real answers — nothing is generic.

Is the content original? ＋

Yes — every question is original, written in Australian English and matched to the authentic style of each exam pathway. Nothing is scraped or recycled from past papers.

Can my child try it without an account? ＋

Yes. Starter sessions run with no sign-in and no card. An account unlocks the intelligence layer: mastery tracking, weekly plans and parent insights.

Does it work on tablets? ＋

MindMosaic runs in any modern browser and is designed responsively for laptops and tablets, with touch-friendly question formats — including drag-and-drop, hotspots and diagrams.
